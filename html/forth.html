Bheem is a leading character of a cartoon series “Chota Bheem”. The director of the show Chota Bheem is Rajiv Chilaka. Bheem is a good boy and he is very strong, his favorite sweet is ladoo made by Tun-Tun Mausi. The king of Dholakpur is Indravarma and his daughter’s name is Princess Indumati. Bheem’s best friends are Raju, Jaggu and Chutki. His enemies are Kalia, Dholu and Bholu. Bheem lives in Dholakpur. Bheem and his friends love to eat ladoos.
